Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Marchin Games ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in our mobile applications.

Information We Collect

Our apps do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. We do not require account creation, and all game data is stored locally on your device.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements, including rewarded video ads and banner ads. AdMob may collect and use certain data such as device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how our apps are used. Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage data such as session duration, screen views, and device type. This data is aggregated and cannot identify individual users. For more information, please refer to Firebase Privacy Information.

In-App Purchases

Our apps offer optional in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain or delete. Game progress is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
